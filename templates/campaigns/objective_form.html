Session Objectives Guide

What are session objectives?

Session objectives are specific goals or tasks for the gaming session. They help track progress and ensure the session stays on track.

Tips:

  • Keep objectives specific and achievable
  • Mark objectives as completed during or after the session
  • Add completion notes to document how objectives were achieved
  • Use objectives to plan future sessions based on completion status
